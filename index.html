Our Story

From a quiet corner of New Row, with love.

Kristopher and Chloe opened the doors at the end of 2023 with a simple idea: cook the kind of food you'd cook at home if you had all the time in the world, pour the kind of drinks you'd never bother making yourself, and let the dog come too.

What started as a tiny bistro has grown around its garden (now the largest in town) and a pair of private rooms named after the buttons and threads that gave the building its name.

"We didn't set out to open a restaurant. We set out to open the place we wished was around the corner from us." Kris & Chloe

The Garden

Our quiet patch of green, the largest in town.

Tucked away behind the bistro is the biggest beer garden in Dunfermline. Family-friendly. Dog-friendly. Never bookable. Just turn up: bring the kids, bring the dog, we'll bring the cocktails.

Open whenever the bistro is. First-come, first-served, always.
